## Mock Mirrors

A key component of the mass simulator is the mock mirror set. These aren't optical-quality surfacesâ€”they're mass and stiffness representatives designed to replicate the dynamic behavior of our flight mirrors during vibration testing.

![Mock mirror front](/images/blog-first-parts/mirror-front.png)

The mock mirrors match the mass distribution and mounting interfaces of the flight design, giving us accurate modal response without the cost and lead time of optical fabrication.
